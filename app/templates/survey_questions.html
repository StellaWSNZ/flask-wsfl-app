  • 1: 1- Poor: I do not connect activities to real-world open water scenarios or explain their relevance. Students struggle to understand the practical importance of the skills being taught.
  • 2: 2- Fair: I make minimal connections to real-world open water scenarios and provide limited explanations of their relevance. Some students may find it challenging to see how the skills apply in practical situations.
  • 3: 3- Good: I make some connections between activities and real-world open water scenarios and explain the relevance of the skills. Most students understand how the skills relate to practical situations, though some may need additional clarification.
  • 4: 4- Very Good: I effectively connect activities to real-world open water scenarios and provide clear explanations of their relevance. Students generally grasp the practical importance of the skills and can relate them to various open water situations.
